*** Readable Passphrase Generator ***

Generates gramatically correct passphrases that are easy for human to remember and hard for computers to guess.

By Murray Grant

For further details see
https://bitbucket.org/ligos/readablepassphrasegenerator

To run try:

PS> PassphraseGenerator.exe

OR

$ dotnet PassphraseGenerator.dll

OR 

$ mono PassphraseGenerator.exe
